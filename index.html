FAQ

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know. Can't find an answer? Just ask →

How long does it take to deliver a video?+
Turnaround depends on the project scope. Typically 5–7 days per batch. Rush projects? Reach out and we'll make it happen.
Do you handle scripting too?+
Yes — scripting is included. I write hook-first scripts designed to capture attention in the first 3 seconds and keep viewers watching to the end.
What is an AI Avatar and do I need one?+
An AI avatar is a digital version of you that can present content without you ever going on camera. Perfect for scaling output, consistency, and multilingual content. Not essential — but a serious growth lever.
How many revisions are included?+
I don't stop until you're genuinely happy with every frame. Revisions are part of the process — not a paywall.
Can I start with a single project?+
Absolutely. A Discovery Call is always free — we'll figure out what structure makes the most sense for where you're at.
What platforms do you optimize for?+
Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, and full-length YouTube. Every deliverable is correctly formatted and sized for each platform.
